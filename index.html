What I Do

My journey into web development began with a passion for graphic design, where I discovered the power of creating both beautiful and functional experiences. Transitioning into a Full Stack Developer role, I focused on mastering JavaScript and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js). Now, as a junior developer, I love bringing ideas to life, whether I’m building backend systems or designing intuitive front-end interfaces. I’m detail-oriented and enjoy collaborating with teams, where I can contribute to and learn from others. What motivates me most is using technology to solve real-world problems and creating solutions that have a positive impact.

Who I Am

I’ve always been drawn to creativity and problem-solving, which led me to earn a certificate in graphic design. After years of managing bars and retail stores, I decided to shift my focus and turn my passion for technology into a career. This brought me to V School, where I recently graduated as a Full Stack Web Developer. Now, I’m channeling my skills into building dynamic web applications that blend design and functionality. I thrive in collaborative environments and am excited to continue learning and growing in this new chapter of my professional journey.
