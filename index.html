What I Do

As a Full Stack Web Developer, I bring a versatile skill set and a creative design background to deliver dynamic, user-centered applications. Proficient in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), I excel in both frontend and backend development, integrating API functionality and deploying projects across platforms. My expertise in UX/UI principles ensures intuitive and visually compelling interfaces, enhanced by my graphic design proficiency with Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Currently expanding my skills in Next.js, TypeScript, and OpenAI technologies, I am driven by the opportunity to create impactful solutions that blend innovation, aesthetics, and seamless functionality.

Who I Am

My creative journey began in 6th grade with a calligraphy project that sparked a lifelong passion for design. Dubbed a "Free Spirit" by my teacher, I embraced thinking outside the box and infusing creativity into every role—from retail management to bar operations and freelancing. Like Dorothy in The Wizard of Oz, my curiosity led me to look behind the curtain, uncovering the code that brings designs to life. This inspired my transition to Full Stack Web Development at V School, where I mastered JavaScript and the MERN stack. Today, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology, building solutions that are both functional and visually compelling. As a team player and collaborative problem-solver, I’m driven by the power of teamwork, continuous learning, and creating impactful results together.
