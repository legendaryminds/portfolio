What I Do

As a Full Stack Web Developer, I combine creativity with technical expertise to craft applications that are both functional and visually compelling. My journey into the world of design began in elementary school, sparking a lifelong passion for visual storytelling. This passion led me to earn a graphic design certification, which I now integrate with my technical skills in JavaScript and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) to build robust, user-friendly applications. With a strong foundation in UX/UI principles, I ensure that each project I work on delivers an intuitive and engaging user experience. My proficiency in Adobe tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign enhances my ability to create interfaces that are not just functional but also aesthetically pleasing. I am driven by the opportunity to solve real-world problems through technology, always with a focus on collaboration and delivering impactful, user-centric solutions from start to finish.

Who I Am

My creative journey began in my 6th-grade classroom, where I discovered my love for design through the art of Calligraphy. My teacher called me a "Free Spirit," recognizing my passion for exploring new ideas and thinking outside the box. This early experience sparked a lifelong love for design, leading me to earn a graphic design certification and weave my skills into every role I’ve taken on—from managing retail stores to leading bar operations and freelancing. But like Dorothy in The Wizard of Oz, my curiosity pushed me to pull back the curtain and see what was behind the screen. I wanted to understand the technology that brought designs to life, which led me to V School. There, I transitioned into Full Stack Web Development, focusing on JavaScript and the MERN stack. Today, I thrive in environments where I can blend my creative instincts with technical expertise, continuously learning, collaborating, and creating solutions that are as beautiful as they are functional.
